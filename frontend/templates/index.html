Intelligent Coordination Engine

Eight Pillars of Smart Allocation

From scattered field reports to measurable impact — every step automated, prioritized, and transparent.

📥

Multi-Source Intake

Aggregate reports from mobile, field teams, surveys, WhatsApp, calls, and community leaders into one system.

🧠

AI Classification

NLP engine categorizes each report into 10+ categories — food, medical, shelter, education, disaster, and more.

⚡

Urgency Scoring

Weighted 8-factor algorithm considers severity, vulnerability, time sensitivity, and fairness to rank critical needs first.

🗺️

Hotspot Detection

K-means++ and DBSCAN clustering identify geographic need concentrations, underserved zones, and response gaps.

🎯

Volunteer Matching

7-factor scoring matches volunteers by skill, proximity, language, availability, reliability, and workload balance.

📦

Resource Allocation

Track food kits, medicines, transport, shelters, and teaching materials. Suggest optimal distribution routes.

📋

Workflow Tracking

Full 9-stage lifecycle from reported → verified → assigned → completed → validated with real-time status updates.

📈

Impact Analytics

Investor-grade dashboards — response trends, completion rates, area-wise impact, beneficiary stats, and more.
